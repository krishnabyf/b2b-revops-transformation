# Sales Pipeline Design

## Objective

Create a standardized commercial pipeline for B2B beverage ingredient sales.

---

# Pipeline Stages

1. New Lead
2. Marketing Qualified Lead
3. Discovery Call
4. Product Sampling
5. Pricing Discussion
6. Procurement Review
7. Trial Order
8. Active Customer
9. Expansion Opportunity
10. Renewal

---

# Stage Definitions

## New Lead
Inbound or outbound lead captured.

## MQL
Lead meets qualification criteria.

## Discovery Call
Commercial discussion scheduled.

## Product Sampling
Samples delivered to customer.

## Pricing Discussion
Commercial pricing negotiation.

## Procurement Review
Customer procurement validation.

## Trial Order
Initial order placed.

## Active Customer
Customer actively purchasing.

## Expansion
Cross-sell or upsell opportunity.

## Renewal
Contract renewal phase.
